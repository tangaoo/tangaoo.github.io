## 1. a/an 

* a + word(beginning with a consonant).
    * a book
    * a word

* an + word(beginning with a vowel).
    * an egg ( **NOTICE. pronounce like a word** ) 
    * an apple 

## 2. plural

* -es, nouns end in **-ch, -sh, -x, -s**.
    * churches
    * brushes
    * foxes
    * dresses

* -s, almostly other nouns.

* -es, special nouns
    * potatoes
    * tomatoes
    * volcanoes
